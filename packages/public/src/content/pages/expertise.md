### ERS Pension Planning

We know the Hawaii Employees' Retirement System inside and out — Contributory I, Contributory II, Hybrid, and Noncontributory plans. We help you understand your benefit calculation, choose the right payment option, and plan for the income gap between your pension and your retirement needs.

### 403(b) Plans for Educators

As a DOE-approved vendor representative, we help educators select, enroll in, and manage their 403(b) supplemental retirement savings plans. We offer fixed annuities (SPDA/FPDA), fixed indexed annuities, and can explain the differences between each option.

### Life Insurance with Living Benefits

We specialize in life insurance products that do more than provide a death benefit. Our policies include Accelerated Benefit Riders (ABR) that allow you to access benefits during your lifetime for chronic, critical, or terminal illness — providing a safety net that traditional long-term care insurance cannot match.

### EUTF Health Insurance Guidance

We help you understand your Employer-Union Health Benefits Trust Fund (EUTF) coverage, plan for the transition to retiree health insurance, and navigate the Medicare enrollment process.
